Event DJ

DJ for birthdays, corporate events, Christmas parties and other events.

TOSMIX has long experience as an "open format" DJ, which means that he can switch seamlessly between genres and timeperiods - depending on what the audience, mood, and situation requires.

If you have booked a venue with professional sound equipment and DJ system, he can use that. Otherwise, he can bring his own to your venue.

Do not hesitate to contact TOSMIX for a chat about your needs via the contact form or social media channels linked to below.
