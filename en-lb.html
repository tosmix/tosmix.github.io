Lønningsbeats

Lønningsbeats is a DJ concept where TOSMIX plays smooth beats when it is payday Friday - in Norway this is typically the Friday closest to the 12th of each month.

The concept is tried and tested since the spring of 2022 with positive feedback. The atmosphere is great when TOSMIX delivers a warmup set to start the Friday right, playing a balanced mix of new and classic tunes in hip hop and r&b land (maybe even disco/nu disco if that's what people are in the mood for).

Running time is typically 3-4 hours starting around 4 PM on Friday afternoon, but it is really up to you what time would suit you the best.

  • Do you want to do something special for your employees? Book TOSMIX for "Lønningsbeats" in your cafeteria or other suitable venue
  • Do you want a competitive edge for your bar or cafe when people are going out for payday Friday? Book TOSMIX for "Lønningsbeats" in your venue

Do not hesitate to contact TOSMIX for a chat about your needs via the contact form or social media channels linked to below.
